ABOUT.MD

ARUTALA was born from a single insight: when the 2021 Javanese Script Congress planned to expand aksara usage in Yogyakarta's public spaces, most people — including native Javanese — couldn't read it.

"Kita aja orang Jawa nggak semua bisa baca, apalagi orang luar daerah atau turis asing." — Marchel Shevchenko

After 2.5 years of research and data collection (powering IndoLLNet), the team built the full app MVP in just 1.5 months. Within weeks of BETA launch, Arutala recorded 300,000+ user activities, with over 60% from outside Indonesia — USA, Singapore, China, and Hong Kong.

◈
AI Handwriting Recognition
Write any Nusantara character and receive instant AI feedback. Powered by IndoLLNet CNN — the same 95–98% accuracy engine built from 2.5 years of handwriting dataset collection.
⊞
Interactive Learning Path
Gamified learning flow inspired by Duolingo — progress from basic characters to full words, diacritics, and conjuncts. Each script type has its own structured curriculum.
◎
5 Scripts, 1 Platform
Javanese, Sundanese (Baku), Balinese, Lampung, and Batak (Mandailing) — all in one app. No other platform covers this breadth of Nusantara scripts with AI-assisted practice.
